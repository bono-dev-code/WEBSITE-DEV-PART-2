Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

MeatMasters Butchery was first established in 2017 as a modest family-run business selling premium meat in Limpopo. The company broadened its product line to focus on premium meats like beef, chicken, lamb, goat, and pork as the market for expertly prepared, locally sourced cuts increased. MeatMasters Butchery now provides expertly cut, ethically sourced meats to homes, businesses, and individuals.

Our Mission

To offer the best quality meat products by mixing traditional meat selections with common domestic cuts, all the while supporting ethical sourcing and regional culinary customs.
